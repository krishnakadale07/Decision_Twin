HOW IT WORKS

Four tools. One clearer decision.

Every part of Decision Twin exists to answer one question: what does the evidence actually say about your options?

Compare

Put two or more paths — careers, colleges, courses, certifications — side by side on the same criteria: salary, demand, skills, and time to get there. No more juggling ten open browser tabs.

Industry Trends

See where hiring is actually growing — job openings, salary movement, emerging skills, and demand by region — updated regularly so your decision is based on today's market, not last year's.

ROI Calculator

Weigh tuition, certification fees, and living costs against expected salary growth. Get a straightforward break-even timeline, so you know not just what something costs, but when it pays off.

AI Mentor

Ask in plain language — "should I do a master's or start working?" — and get a clear breakdown of pros, cons, risk, and alternatives, built from the same data as the rest of the platform.
